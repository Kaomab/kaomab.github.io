Information About me

As the Founder and CEO of the KUA Initiative, I am passionate about advocating for refugees with disabilities. My ultimate goal is to create a world where everyone has access to supportive and inclusive environments that empower them to live their best lives despite the challenges they face. I am committed to shining a light on the often-overlooked issues affecting refugees with disabilities and developing innovative solutions to enhance their quality of life and integration into society. My technical expertise as a web developer and electrical wiring engineer allows me to leverage the power of technology to create sustainable solutions that make a real difference in people's lives. Being a member of the YALI Network, I am part of a community of leaders who share my commitment to driving positive change across Africa and beyond. I believe that by working together and collaborating with like-minded professionals and organizations, we can truly make a difference in the lives of those who need it most. I am inspired every day by the resilience and strength of the people I work with, and I am grateful for the opportunity to make a meaningful impact in their lives.
